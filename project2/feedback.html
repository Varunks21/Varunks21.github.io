BIT has achieved a tremendous success in placements of its UG and PG students across all programs. Every year more than 150 top notch companies visit our Institution and conduct placement activities for the full-time recruitment. The Training and Placement Centre takes immense pleasure in stating that around 70 percent of eligible students are placed in their dream companies with attractive packages. The average compensation is Rs 6.5 LPA and highest compensation is Rs 37.5 LPA during 22-23.

Since it's inception in 2020 as a department ,the department of AIML has put in significant effort in ensuring that our students are being provided with every material and source necessary for their placement preparation owing to this our students have reaped in significant dividends by getting placed in good companies.
